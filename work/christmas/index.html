2024 3D Animation

Happy Christmas

This 3D animation focuses on the theme of Christmas, featuring the chracter from COZISOOP, Sori, and stylized 3D assets that evoke the festive spirit. To amplify the celebratory atmosphere, I incorporated a dynamic snow-covered background and a variety of Christmas-themed elements. The character's engaging interaction with the camera draws viewers into the scene, fostering a sense of immersion and evoking audience reactions. The integration of sound further enhances the narrative, creating a seamless and engaging audiovisual experience.
