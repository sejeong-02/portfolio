2024 Merchandise Design

Rockstar keychains

The project focused on character and merchandise design, incorporating elements of guitar motifs and pop-culture aesthetics. Inspired by the K-pop group Tomorrow by Together, each of the five characters was distinctly designed with unique facial features and personalities, reflected in the pose and color of their guitar. From product design to packaging, the goal was to deliver a cohesive and enjoyable experience for buyers, blending visual storytelling with engaging, high-quality merchandise.
