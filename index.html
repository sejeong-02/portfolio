About Me

Sejeong Park is a Bay Area merchandise and visual designer, with a passion for blending visual aesthetics and user experience. Her work spans a diverse range of projects, from launching original art initiatives to bringing her designs to market. Sejeong’s portfolio reflects her commitment to creating impactful, engaging artwork that resonates with audiences both in digital and consumer spaces. Sejeong strives to create aesthetic and interactive practical products that people can enjoy and connect emotionally with the designs. She is an enthusiastic digital media artist who forms playful and interactive experiences by utilizing physical objects to express her artistic expressions.
